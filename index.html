Kharif crops

Cotton

Cotton is a cash crop. This is a Kharif crop. Its origin is India. It is a member of the Malvaceae family. Its 2 types are found in the world. The first is known as native cotton and the second is known as American cotton and (Barwadens). Cotton is prepared from this, which is called white gold.Cotton plants are perennial, shrub like trees. Whose length is 2-7 feet. The flowers are white or pale yellow. The fruits of cotton are called balls, which are smooth and greenish yellow in color; bractioles are covered with thorns. There are seeds and cotton inside the fruit. Black soil is required for cotton crop production. The highest cotton production in India is in the state of Gujarat. Clothes made of cotton are called cotton clothes. Cotton mainly consists of cellulose.

Rice

Rice is the most important food crop of India covering about one-fourth of the total cropped area and providing food to about half of the Indian population. Punjab has made tremendous progress in rice productivity and production during the past 45 years. Due to use of high yielding varieties and new technology Punjab has given the title of "Rice Bowl of India".The traditional method of rice cultivation is to water the fields at the time of planting the young seedling, or later. This simple method requires good irrigation planning, but reduces the growth of less vigorous weeds and pest plants,Rice is prevalent in South India and East-South India more than in North India.

Sugarcane

Sugarcane (Sugarcane; Botanical name - Saccharum officinarum) is a major cash crop of the world, from which sugar, jaggery, etc. are manufactured. Brazil is the largest producer of sugarcane and India ranks second in the world in terms of sugarcane productivity. Sugarcane farming provides employment to a large number of people and plays an important role in earning foreign exchange.Sugarcane juice is crushed and crushed to prepare various substances, such as jaggery, sugar, sugar candy, sugar, etc. There is a difference in the properties of these substances as well.
